Greek Philosophy

Socrates

Father of Western philosophy; teacher of inquiry and introspection.
Plato

Founder of the Academy; philosopher of forms and idealism.
Aristotle

Master of logic, ethics, and scientific methodology.
Heraclitus

Philosopher of change — “You cannot step into the same river twice.”
Epictetus

Stoic teacher of discipline, resilience, and inner freedom.
Marcus Aurelius

Stoic emperor emphasizing responsibility, clarity, and virtue.
