Ibn Arabi

About Ibn Arabi

Ibn Arabi, known as *Shaykh al-Akbar* (“The Greatest Master”), is one of the most influential mystics in Islamic history. His teachings revolve around the *Unity of Existence* — the idea that all creation is a manifestation of the Divine Reality.

He beautifully expressed that God is not separate from the world; everything is a mirror reflecting the One. His wisdom blends poetry, metaphysics, and deep mystical insight.

Five Famous Quotes

“My heart has become capable of every form.”

True spiritual openness embraces all paths leading to the Divine.

“He who knows himself knows his Lord.”

Self-discovery is the doorway to discovering God.

“The universe is an imagination within the Divine.”

Existence is a symbolic unfolding of God’s attributes.

“Wherever you turn, there is the Face of God.”

The Divine is present in every moment, place, and being.

“The ignorant one sees multiplicity; the knower sees only One.”

Separation is illusion; unity is truth.
