Plato

About Plato

Plato, the student of Socrates and teacher of Aristotle, transformed philosophy into a systematic discipline. His Academy — the first university in the Western world — shaped education, politics, metaphysics, and ethics for millennia.

He believed in an unseen world of perfect “Forms,” of which our physical world is only a shadow. True knowledge, according to Plato, is remembering these eternal truths and aligning one’s soul with them.
