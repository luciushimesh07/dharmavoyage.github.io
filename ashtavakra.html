Ashtavakra

About Ashtavakra

Ashtavakra, the sage behind the Ashtavakra Gita, taught one of the purest forms of Advaita philosophy — direct, uncompromising non-duality. His teaching discards rituals, techniques, and scriptures, guiding one straight into the truth of pure awareness.

Five Quotes & Their Meanings

1. "You are the solitary witness of all that is; cease to identify with the mind."

The Self is not thoughts — it is the awareness behind them.

2. "If you detach from the body and rest in consciousness, you will be instantly happy."

True joy is not from circumstances but from disidentifying with the body.

3. "The wise man knows himself to be the Self and is not afraid of death."

Fear only exists when we mistake the body for who we are.

4. "You are not the doer; actions happen in nature."

Seeing life as a flow removes ego, guilt, and pride.

5. "Liberation is your nature — nothing binds you except your own imagination."

Bondage is a misunderstanding; clarity returns you to freedom.
