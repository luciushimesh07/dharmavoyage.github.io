Acharya Prashant

About Acharya Prashant

Acharya Prashant is a contemporary spiritual teacher known for clarity, directness, and dismantling psychological illusions. His teachings focus on freedom from ego, addiction to pleasure, false identities, and escapism. He blends Vedanta with sharp modern insight.

Five Quotes & Meanings

1. "Understand the mind, and it loses the power to trouble you."

Suffering dissolves when the mind’s patterns are exposed.

2. "Real love is intelligence."

Love is clarity, not emotional dependence.

3. "Freedom is not comfort — it is responsibility."

Growth demands facing truth, not escape.

4. "Pleasure binds; understanding liberates."

Awareness breaks the cycle of craving.

5. "Truth begins where the ego ends."

The ego blocks clear perception of reality.
