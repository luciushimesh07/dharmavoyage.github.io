Osho

About Osho

Osho was a contemporary mystic known for breaking boundaries between spirituality and personal freedom. He emphasized meditation, awareness, love, celebration, and living with total intensity. His teachings dissolve guilt, conditioning, and religious rigidity.

Five Quotes & Meanings

1. “Be — don’t try to become.”

Transformation comes from presence, not effort toward an identity.

2. “Truth is not something outside to be discovered — it is inside to be realized.”

Awakening is inward, not a search in the world.

3. “Courage is a love affair with the unknown.”

One must embrace uncertainty to grow spiritually.

4. “A certain darkness is needed to see the stars.”

Difficulty can become the ground for awakening.

5. “If you love a flower, don’t pick it.”

Real love allows freedom — it doesn’t possess.
