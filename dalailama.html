Dalai Lama

About the Dalai Lama

The 14th Dalai Lama is the global voice of Tibetan Buddhism. He teaches compassion, universal responsibility, inner peace, and non-violence. His wisdom blends ancient Buddhist insight with modern humanitarian values.

Five Quotes & Their Meanings

1. “Be kind whenever possible. It is always possible.”

Compassion is never limited by circumstance.

2. “Happiness is not something ready-made; it comes from your own actions.”

Your daily choices shape your emotional world.

3. “Love and compassion are necessities, not luxuries.”

Human well-being depends on connection.

4. “Our prime purpose in life is to help others.”

Serving others fulfills our deepest potential.

5. “If you want others to be happy, practice compassion.”

Compassion transforms relationships and society.
