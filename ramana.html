Ramana Maharshi

About Ramana Maharshi

Ramana Maharshi is one of the greatest sages of modern India, known for his direct method of Self-inquiry: "Who am I?". His presence radiated silence, and his teaching pointed directly to the experience of pure awareness beyond mind and identity.

Five Quotes & Their Meanings

1. "Your own Self is the ultimate teacher."

The true guru is within — the Self reveals all truth.

2. "The mind is only thoughts. Stop thinking and show me the mind."

He reveals that the mind has no independent existence.

3. "Happiness is your nature."

Joy is not acquired — it is uncovered.

4. "The world is seen when the mind rises; it disappears when the mind subsides."

Perception depends on mental activity — silence reveals reality.

5. "Self-inquiry is the most direct path."

Asking “Who am I?” dissolves the false ego and reveals the Self.
