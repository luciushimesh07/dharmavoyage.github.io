Heraclitus

About Heraclitus

Heraclitus, the philosopher of perpetual change, believed that reality is a river of constant transformation. He emphasized opposites, tension, and the dynamic movement that creates harmony.

His teachings laid the foundation for metaphysics, dialectics, and the idea that struggle and contradiction shape the world.

Five Famous Quotes

“You cannot step into the same river twice.”

Everything flows; nothing stays the same.

“The only constant in life is change.”

Transformation is the nature of existence.

“Opposition brings concord.”

Harmony arises from the tension of opposites.

“Character is destiny.”

Inner nature shapes fate.

“It is in changing that we find purpose.”

Growth is the engine of meaning.
