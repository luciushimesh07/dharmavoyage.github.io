Swami Vivekananda

About Vivekananda

Swami Vivekananda was a global icon of strength, fearlessness, and spiritual clarity. He represented Vedanta and Yoga to the modern world, emphasizing service, discipline, and discovering one’s inner divinity through effort and character.

Five Quotes & Meanings

1. “Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached.”

True progress demands tireless effort and focus.

2. “You are the creator of your own destiny.”

Strength comes from responsibility and self-mastery.

3. “All power is within you; you can do anything.”

The divine potential lives inside every individual.

4. “Strength is life; weakness is death.”

Growth demands courage and resilience.

5. “The greatest religion is to be true to your own nature.”

Authenticity leads to spiritual success.
